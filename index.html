Australia has turned poker machines into a quiet national drain.

Every year, billions of dollars disappear through pokies in pubs and clubs across the country. The losses are so constant they stop feeling visible — but they add up with brutal speed.

What looks like ordinary entertainment on the floor of a venue has become one of the biggest and most persistent transfers of money out of Australian households.
